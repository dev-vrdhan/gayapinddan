Our Pillars of Faith

How we combine ancient Vedic shastra systems with modern comfort and transparency.

Family Lineage & Tradition

Our ancestors have served as pilgrim guides (Pandas) since the Maratha restoration eras. We possess authentic regional registers (Khatas) where your forefathers signed.

Verified Panda Ji

Every priest executing the ritual belongs to the recognized Gaya Panda Committee. We follow authentic Vedic mantras (Sankalpa Vidhi) without shortcuts.

Transparent Process

No hidden charges, sudden demands for extra dakshina, or unnecessary puja add-ons. We detail costs and samagri list before you set travel plans.

Video Call Support

For elderly relatives or global devotees unable to travel to Bihar, we arrange complete interactive online rituals with live audio-video streaming.

Multiple Languages

Puja can be explained and conducted in Hindi, English, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, and Kannada. We bridge linguistic gaps for absolute comfort.

24x7 Support Desk

Our pilgrim desk helps arrange local transport, pick-ups from Gaya junction railway station, hotel bookings, and handles queries at any hour.

Verified Authority & Temple Association

Official registrations and heritage certificates confirming authentic priesthood status.

When you perform a ritual as deep as Pind Daan, it must be led by a priest who possesses scriptural authority and direct access to the sacred spots of Gaya Dham. Our group is registered with the Gaya Panda Committee and has heritage status at the Vishnupad Temple Management.

Our panda family holds the physical, hand-written Khata Registers (Vansh Vriksha Khatas) dating back hundreds of years. These registers map out genealogies of millions of families across different states. When you arrive, we help identify the exact page where your grandfather or great-grandfather signed decades ago, reinforcing a direct lineage connection.
